- **机制**：unlink操作会执行`*(P->fd + 0x18) = P->bk`和`*(P->bk + 0x10) = P->fd`
- **实际效果**：
  - `*(0x602138 + 0x18) = 0x602140` → `0x602150 = 0x602138`
  - `*(0x602140 + 0x10) = 0x602138` → `0x602150 = 0x602138`
